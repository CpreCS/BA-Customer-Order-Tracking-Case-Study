# Epic: Customer Order Tracking Portal

## Description
Provide customers with a self-service order tracking solution to enable visibility into order status, reduce reliance on support, and improve overall customer experience.

---

## Business Objectives
- Reduce support call volume  
- Improve customer satisfaction  
- Increase customer retention  

---

## Constraints
- Backend system updates twice daily  
- 8-week delivery timeline  
- Must integrate with existing infrastructure  

---

## Out of Scope (Phase 1)
- Real-time tracking  
- Order cancellations  
- Email notifications
